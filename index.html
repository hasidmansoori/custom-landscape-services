Our Fleet

Equipment to tackle landscaping, maintenance, and dirt work. Highlights of our fleet include:

  • Big John tree spade truck capable of transplanting trees up to 8' diameter
  • Tight access Vermeer tree spade
  • Implements to fulfill most landscape requirements from mowing to plowing and planting
  • Specialized horse track planer, road grader, and snow plow

The Farm

We operate a tree farm in Northeast Arkansas where we plant and nurture native seedlings. Trees include bald cypress, various species of pine, hardwood trees, nut trees like pecan and black walnut, and many types of oak. Ornamentals like dogwood, redbud and various evergreens and junipers. We also have a small fruit orchard with varieties of apple, plum, peach, and fig.
